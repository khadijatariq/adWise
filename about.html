About Us

adWise is a platform for Computer Science majors at LUMS which aims to guide students to choose courses by filtering based on prerequisites, clashes and type of fields in Computer Science.

- Perspective

At the start of each semester, students have to spend countless hours planning their courses according to their requirements. In most cases, later, they realise that they have clashes or they do not fulfil the prerequisite for a course. Even worse, students might find out that the course they took has a strict instructor or is difficult so they end up dropping it. adWise is a new system that is a replacement for certain aspects of three existing systems used by students in these critical times: Zambeel and Suraj.

- Scope

A web application which aims to guide the undergraduate CS majors at LUMS to choose the right courses. By filtering out the courses based on prerequisites and clashes, the application provides each student with a clear choice of courses in each field. adWise also displays courses being offered in the upcoming semester including their schedule and course outline.
Not only that, it suggests career options and instructors as advisers according to a particular student’s field of interest. Students can check and post reviews about a particular course or instructor. The idea is to contain all the important and crucial functionality of Suraj, Portal and LDF on a single platform, thus, making the task of choosing a course easier and hassle-free.

- Users and Characteristics

adWise will cater mainly to CS students and instructors. Although the application will not differentiate between them, the students can further be divided into two groups according to their requirements i.e. Freshmen, Sophomores and Juniors, Seniors.

Freshmen, Sophomores: At this stage, students are mostly in need of guidance in their career paths. adWise helps the students by referring them to instructors working in that field. In case the student is indecisive about his field, it also suggests courses in a variety of fields to help him in his decision. Since it is advised to enroll in pre-decided core courses for the first four semesters, students do not face much difficulty during enrollment.

Juniors, Seniors: At the start of each of the last four semesters, students face challenges in deciding the courses to enroll in. adWise will provide course outlines for all the courses offered in that semester. It will also suggest a list of possible courses in each field that can fit into that student’s schedule. Not only that, the students will be able to post and look at the reviews of a particular course and instructor.

Instructors: Instructors have limited usage of this application. adWise will allow them to update their information, courses and course outlines. They can also view the evaluations and reviews of their courses by students.
