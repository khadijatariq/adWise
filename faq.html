What is adWise?

A web application which aims to guide the undergraduate CS majors at LUMS to choose the right courses. By filtering out the courses based on prerequisites and clashes, the application provides each student with a clear choice of courses in each field. adWise also displays courses being offered in the upcoming semester including their schedule and course outline.
Not only that, it suggests career options and instructors as advisers according to a particular student’s field of interest. Students can check and post reviews about a particular course or instructor. The idea is to contain all the important and crucial functionality of Suraj, Portal and LDF on a single platform, thus, making the task of choosing a course easier and hassle-free.
